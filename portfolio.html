My Portfolio


Ice Cream Maker

The Ice Cream Maker is a game where the customer can customize their ice cream. The game asks the user what flavor of ice cream they would like from a selection. They also get to pick their toppings from a selection as well.


Turtle Race Game

The Turtle Race Game is between two turtles, in which one is controlled by the user. The user gets to control the movement of turtle and at the end of the game, a leaderboard is displayed with the order of winners.


Minion Catcher

The Minion Catcher is a game where the user gets to control the movements of Gru to catch the minions without getting caught by the purple ones. If they do that they win, but if they get caught they lose.


PLTW 3.2.4
